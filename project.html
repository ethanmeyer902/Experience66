Project Description

As we approach the 100-year anniversary of Route 66, an important question arises: how can we effectively showcase its history, landmarks, and everything it has to offer? Our team has decided to develop a solution that enables users to experience the rich history of Route 66 through a dedicated mobile application.

Solution

To address this challenge, our team was tasked with creating a mobile application that provides seamless access to the history, data, and cultural significance of Route 66. The app will allow users to explore this information at their fingertipsâ€”whether stationary or on the move. A key feature will be geofencing, which enhances the experience by triggering location-specific content as users travel along Route 66.

Technologies

The following tools and platforms will be used to implement the project:

  • ArcGIS: Used for the map component of the app, providing location-based services and interactive mapping. This solution was recommended by our client.
  • Java: Chosen as the primary programming language because of its compatibility with ArcGIS and suitability for mobile app development.
  • GitHub: Our version control and collaborative code-sharing platform, enabling efficient teamwork and project management.
  • NAU Archives: Serving as our database pool, from which we will pull historical data and resources related to Route 66.
